About Me

I am Molly and I am a full stack software developer with a love for elegant design and user experience. My passion lies in the creative process; in art, writing, and now coding! I've always found creative problem solving to be satisfying, connecting the dots and viewing problems from different angles. Finding my passion for coding felt like a natural step in my love for being a creator and designer.

My personal hobbies include all types of tabletop gaming, primarily rpgs and board games. I love creating art of all sorts, digital, watercolor, etc. I love being able to share things I create with my friends and family.

I'm excited to start a new chapter in my life as a professional developer!
